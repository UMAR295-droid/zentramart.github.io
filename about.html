About ShopTalk

We are passionate e-commerce practitioners sharing what actually works.

Our Mission

ShopTalk was born out of frustration. Too many e-commerce blogs offer generic, recycled advice — the kind that sounds good but never moves the needle for real store owners.

We started this blog in 2017 with a simple promise: every article we publish is tested, data-backed, and actionable. No fluff. No filler. Just strategies that work in the real world.

Today we help over 50,000 online store owners every month with content that covers everything from SEO and email marketing to Shopify optimization and pricing psychology.
